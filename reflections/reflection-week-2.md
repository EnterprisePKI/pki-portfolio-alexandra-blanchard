Week 2 Reflection

This week, I learned about symmetric encryption, hashing, digital signatures, and the difference between confidentiality, integrity, and authenticity.

This week's challenge revolves around this week's vocabulary and how it aligns with financial regulations:
1. PCI DSSv4.0 req 3.6 - HSM’s making symmetric encryption tamper-proof.
2. SOX 302/404 - Hashing and state roots and their role in making sure CFO collect information to integrate into their business intelligence system.
3. PSD2 SCA - Reinforce digital signatures through the requirements of customer transactions.

These concepts appear in real-world regulatory systems in the form of cryptography controls in banking enterprises using Thales Luna HSM (FIPS-140-3 Level 3) to certify crypto execution.

The question for this week is, what does reinforcing digital signatures entail?
